.

Growth and Expansion: In the 1920s and 1930s, the rental car industry began to grow as more people started traveling for leisure and business. Companies like Hertz, founded in 1918 by Walter L. Jacobs, and Avis, founded in 1946 by Warren Avis, became prominent players in the industry.

Post-War Boom: After World War II, the rental car industry experienced significant growth, fueled by the rise of tourism and business travel. This period saw the expansion of existing companies and the emergence of new players in the market.
